MORE THAN A NUMBER

More Than A Number is a data visualization of American adults with depression. According to the 2020 National Survey on Drug Use and Health, 8.4% of Americans aged 18 and older experienced at least one depressive episode between 2019-2020. Though this may not seem like a high number, this data reflects real people and real struggles. More Than A Number strives to further humanize and contextualize this statistic by displaying the proportion of Americans with depression amongst a user-specified sample of randomly generated profile pictures.

DISCLAIMER: To protect usersâ€™ privacy, none of the people in these photos are real. Instead, all profile images are randomly generated from This Person Does Not Exist, an API that computes hyper-realistic images of fake people. In addition, the photos displayed are in no way representative of measured distributions amongst different demographic groups. Although all the photos used in this visualization are fake, they could just as easily be real.
